Our Firm

Founded in 2016, H N Sirvi & Associates is a Chartered Accountancy firm headquartered in Secunderabad, Telangana. We specialize in delivering tailored and practical solutions in Audit & Assurance, Direct and Indirect Taxation, and cross-border advisory.

Our team of over 16 professionals, led by two experienced partners, brings deep expertise in structuring international investments, transfer pricing, litigation support, and corporate compliance.

What Makes Us Different

  • Hands-on advisory for Joint Ventures and foreign collaborations
  • Expertise in ITAT representation and high-stake tax litigation
  • Integrated consulting across Income Tax, GST, and FEMA
  • Due diligence and valuation support for mergers and investments
  • Focused compliance strategies for both Indian and foreign entities

Our Mission

To provide professional services that combine excellence, integrity, and value, helping our clients achieve financial clarity and regulatory compliance in a complex business environment.
